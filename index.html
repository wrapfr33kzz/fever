Feel Better, Sam! 🌸

🌡️ Check Temperature Regularly

Monitor your fever to make sure it doesn’t get too high.

💧 Stay Hydrated:

Drink plenty of water, herbal tea, or clear broths to stay hydrated.

🛏️ Rest:

Make sure to get plenty of rest. Your body needs energy to fight off the fever.

🌬️ Stay Cool:

Use a fan or cool cloths on your forehead to help reduce your temperature. Dress in light clothing and use a light blanket.

💊 Medication:

Take fever-reducing medication like acetaminophen or ibuprofen, but follow the dosage instructions carefully.

🍲 Eat Light:

If you’re hungry, eat light and easy-to-digest foods like soup, toast, or crackers.

📺 Stay Entertained:

Keep your mind occupied with something relaxing, like watching your favorite show or reading a book.

🛁 Warm Bath:

A lukewarm bath can help reduce fever and make you feel better.

🚨 Seek Help:

If your fever goes above 103°F (39.4°C) or if you have other severe symptoms, call a doctor or seek medical attention immediately.
